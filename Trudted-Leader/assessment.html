Assessment

Assessing Five Essential Leadership Aspects

The DCL Model TM is used with permission from LDN Global

The Trusted Leader 360° Assessment, based on the Lead Develop Care Model (LDC) by the Leader Development Network, evalutes a leader's character and compentence in achieving results and working with people. The LDC Model emphasizes the primary responsibilities to lead, develop, and care for others, along with "Operetional Aspects" of what each responsibility looks like in action. The TL360° offers feedback based on this model and incorporates essential aspects of Emotional Intelligence.

Emotionel Intelligence

If has been show to be the Achilles heel for many leaders. The Knowledge and skills needes in leading are notsufficient. Understanding oneself and others, then responding in healthy and helpful ways is not understood as indispensable. The TL360° takes these aspects of leadership into account and incorporates them into the feedback.

The Result

A comprehensive feedback tool which enables leaders to understand how others experience their leadership and develop a path forward for growth and influence a path forward for growth and influence..
